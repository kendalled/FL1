The Power of Fleat's Patented Technology

Outfitted with StreetLogic™, Fleat’s Mobile Retail Vehicles (MRVs) are custom designed to support a vendor’s operations and holds perishable goods at optimal temperatures. Mobile storefronts are loaded daily with a flexible inventory that reflects ever-changing consumer preferences. Specialized storage, checkout, and workflow equipment allow mobile storefronts to keep going for up to 8 hours, unlike traditional delivery methods. Plus, you get to keep 100% of your revenue with our fixed, monthly fee.

How does StreetLogic™ work?

Customers are Notified.

Mobile storefronts send highly targeted notifications to your customers. Our Artifical Intelligence is always factoring in: location, real-time inventory, and customer habits.

  • More

Order(s) are Placed.

Once paired, customers can select from your real-time inventory. They can customize items, add to their order, and browse your menu. Fleat's B2B solution easily drops into your app or site.

  • More

Delivery and Checkout.

As you arrive, your customer(s) are notified. Previous customers in the area will be notified of your proximity, and can be targeted for alerts as well. All revenue goes to you directly, unlike traditional delivery methods.
